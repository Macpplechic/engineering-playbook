## The short version

I'm direct. I invest heavily in the people on my team. I expect you to be in it with me — not just executing tasks, but owning outcomes, asking hard questions, and telling me when something is wrong. I will do the same for you.

Time matters. Clarity matters. Momentum matters. Everything else is negotiable.
