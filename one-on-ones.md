# How I Run 1:1s

1:1s are your time, not mine. I protect them. I don't cancel them unless something is genuinely on fire.

## What they're for

- Your growth, your blockers, your questions
- Honest conversation about how things are going — for you and for the team
- Feedback in both directions
- The things that don't fit anywhere else

They are not status updates. If you come to a 1:1 and walk me through your ticket queue, we're going to redirect. I can read a Jira board.

## How I run them

I'll ask you to own the agenda. Come with something — even one thing you want to talk about. If you don't know where to start, I'll ask:

- What's going well?
- What's in your way?
- What are you thinking about that you haven't said out loud yet?

I take notes. I follow up on what I say I'll do. I expect the same from you.

## Frequency

Weekly by default. We can adjust based on what you need — more when things are intense, different format when things are stable. But we don't drop them.

## The most important thing

Tell me the truth. I can't help you with a problem I don't know about. If something is wrong — with the work, with the team, with me — say it here. This is the place for it.
